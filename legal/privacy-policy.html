Privacy Policy

Faction Warfare · Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Faction Warfare (“the App”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the App inside Reddit communities through the Devvit platform.

The App runs entirely within Reddit. It does not operate a separate website or account system outside Reddit. By playing, you also agree to Reddit’s User Agreement and Reddit Privacy Policy.

1. Information we collect

When you interact with Faction Warfare, we may process:

  • Reddit account identifiers — your Reddit user ID and username, provided by Reddit through Devvit when you are logged in.
  • Gameplay data — faction assignment, votes cast (including which board tile you selected), clue vetoes, commander actions, turn participation, and match outcomes.
  • Player statistics — wins, losses, vote and clue counts, streaks, and in-game trophies stored in persistent player records.
  • Trust status — whether your account meets subreddit-configured trust rules (for example minimum account age or karma), checked via Reddit’s API to limit voting abuse.
  • Subreddit and post context — which subreddit and war-room post you are viewing, used to load the correct game state.
  • Aggregated telemetry — anonymous counters per subreddit (such as post views, gate entries, votes cast) with no per-user breakdown.

If you browse without logging in, we do not assign you a faction or record personal gameplay actions beyond anonymous aggregate counters.

2. How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Run multiplayer game sessions and resolve turns.
  • Assign factions, enforce one vote per turn, and display your locked-in vote.
  • Track commander roles, clues, and vetoes.
  • Maintain cross-season player statistics shown in the App.
  • Detect abuse and apply trust restrictions configured by moderators.
  • Improve reliability through aggregated, non-identifying usage metrics.

3. Where data is stored

Game state and player records are stored in Devvit Redis on Reddit’s infrastructure. Turn-scoped data expires automatically after a limited retention period. Player statistics persist until you request deletion (see Section 6).

We do not operate our own external database for player accounts.

4. Third-party services

The App may call Datamuse (api.datamuse.com) to fetch word suggestions when building or refreshing word pools. These requests contain word topics only — we do not send your Reddit username, user ID, or other personal information to Datamuse.

Reddit provides authentication, hosting, Redis, realtime updates, and moderator APIs through Devvit. Their policies govern Reddit-side processing.

5. Sharing and sale of data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share personal data with third parties for advertising. Game actions visible to other players (such as aggregate vote counts on tiles) are part of normal gameplay inside the war room.

6. Your choices and data deletion

You can request deletion of your personal Faction Warfare statistics at any time:

  • In a subreddit where the App is installed, open the subreddit menu (⋯) and choose “Faction Warfare: delete my data”.

This scrubs identifying fields from your player record (username is replaced with “[Deleted User]” and trophies are cleared). Aggregate gameplay counters may remain in anonymized form. Turn-level vote records in active or recent seasons may persist in Redis until they expire, but are not linked to a display name after redaction.

For other privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/vladifel/faction-wars.

7. Children

The App is intended for Reddit users who meet Reddit’s minimum age requirements for their region. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
